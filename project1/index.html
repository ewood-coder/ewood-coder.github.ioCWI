Meet The Doctor Behind It All

Dr. Shannon Farris

Dr. Farris wanted to get started in dentistry to provide her patients with a better experience than she was given. (She had braces for seven years during her childhood!) Dr. Farris received her BS in Microbiology from Brigham Young University and graduated with her DMD from University of Louisville School of Dentistry in 2015. After having worked as an associate in other offices, Dr. Farris realized starting her own practice would allow her the freedom to create the dental experience she always envisioned for her patients while being able to spend quality time with her family. Dr. Farris has been happily married to her husband, Joseph, for eleven years and, together, they have four children. Learning new things and interesting facts, spending time outside enjoying the beautiful weather, and making people smile are just a few of Dr. Farris's favorite things to do!

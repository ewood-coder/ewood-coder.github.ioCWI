Locally owned and operated. We are convieniently located on the southwest corner of Eagle Road and Hightop Road. As a full service floral boutique, we love our day to day designs along with creating cohesive custom designs for any special event. Plants, gifts, gift baskets are one of our many specialties. Knowing that sometimes special days get misplaced in our memories :), we are here to help with last minute gift ideas. Our inventory is always rotating, so just give us a call with selection questions.
